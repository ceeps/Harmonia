Living


In Harmonia there is ample living space for all the students of the university. The living areas for all of the students are located in a corner of the town. With all the living areas being close to each other this means that the student accomodation is a very sociable place to live and students enjoy living there. The living space itself is split up into different buildings each housing 8 flats with between 6-8 people in each flat. The rooms that the students live in contain there own bathroom, bed, shelf space and a desk. Each and every student is also guarenteed a new computer in their room every year whilst they are at the university.

The flats also contain fully functional kitchens which contain fridges, freezers, ovens, microwaves, kettles, toasters, dishwashers and also washing/dryer machines. Along with all these appliances there is also a large cupbaord for each student living in the flat. There is also a cleaning cupboard which is where the two cleaning robots are located. These robots will clean the kitchen and the students bedrooms/bathrooms.

Aswell as your room and the kitchen students also get access to the onsite gym, the shop and the bar/club. The gym contains a lot of equipment and is fully staffed throughout the day so if help is required the gym staff will be happy to help out. The shop is fully stocked with a wide range of groceries and recieves deliveries daily.

Another vital part of your living is the accessibility to the internet. At all the accommodation in the town there is a dedicated 50Mbit internet line going into all of the rooms so whatever the students' needs are they will be ok.
